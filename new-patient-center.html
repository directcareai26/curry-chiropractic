What to bring to your first visit

  • Your completed forms. Fill out the New Patient Health History form online or bring the printed copy with you.
  • Photo ID & insurance card. So we can verify your benefits and get you checked in quickly.
  • Any relevant records. Recent X-rays, MRIs, or reports related to your condition, if you have them.
  • A list of your concerns. What questions, concerns, and goals regarding your wellness can we help you with?
