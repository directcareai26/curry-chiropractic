Services & Techniques

Care designed around your body

From the first adjustment to long-term wellness, every visit is tailored to where you are in your healing journey.

Chiropractic Care

Gentle, specific spinal correction that's safe, comfortable, and effective — restoring alignment and relieving pressure on the nervous system.

Thermography

State-of-the-art infrared scanning detects neurological irritation in your spine that could be causing your condition.

Your First Visit

Know exactly what to expect

No surprises — just a warm welcome and a clear path forward. Here's how your first visit flows.

01

Warm Welcome

Our receptionist welcomes you as a member of our family the moment you walk through the door.

02

Consultation

We discuss your health concerns, history, and goals to fully understand your condition and expectations.

03

Examination

A complete chiropractic examination testing your reflexes, flexibility, and range of motion.

04

X-Ray Studies

X-rays help us develop the most effective treatment plan and alert us to any serious spinal conditions.

05

Report of Findings

We answer three questions: how we can help, how often you'll need to visit, and what your care will cost.

06

Treatment & Wellness

This may include adjustments, physical therapy, and soft-tissue massage — plus a wellness program for home.

Phases of Care

A clear path from pain to wellness

Healing happens in stages. We guide you through each one with care.

Phase 1

Relief Care

The first goal is simple: get you out of pain. In this phase we focus on reducing your symptoms and easing discomfort, often with more frequent visits.

Phase 2

Corrective Care

Once the pain eases, we address the root cause — correcting structure and function so the problem doesn't keep coming back.

Phase 3

Wellness Care

With your body restored, periodic visits keep your spine healthy, prevent future issues, and help you feel your best long-term.
